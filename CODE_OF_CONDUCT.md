## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and thoughtful environment for collaboration.

This project exists to explore **serious questions about
control, safety, and responsibility in adaptive systems**.
We expect contributors to engage accordingly.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on ideas, not individuals
- Accept critical feedback professionally
- Acknowledge uncertainty and limits
- Act in good faith

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Dismissive or hostile communication
- Promotion of unsafe or irresponsible AI use
- Deliberate attempts to bypass safety mechanisms
- Treating the system as a game or exploit challenge

---

## Technical Responsibility

Contributors must recognize that:
- This system deals with **powerful adaptive behavior**
- Irresponsible suggestions can cause real harm
- Safety concerns take priority over novelty

We reserve the right to reject contributions that,
while technically interesting, undermine system safety.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may:
- Request changes in tone or behavior
- Close issues or PRs
- Temporarily or permanently restrict participation

Decisions are made to protect the integrity of the project
and the safety of its outcomes.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior,
please contact the maintainers privately.

All reports will be handled with discretion.

---

## Final Note

Adaptive Intelligence is not neutral.

By participating in this project, you agree to uphold
a standard of responsibility that matches the power
of the systems being built.
